SCENE
Climactic moment. Rocket Launch, golden hour side lighting, overwhelming scale.

CAMERA
Drone camera descends from high altitude, rapid upward tilt revealing scene with urgency. Handheld energy throughout. lower third framing.

ACTION
[0–1.5s] Rapid drone dive — drone dives from cloud cover at extreme velocity before the subject below can be named or identified, dark cool cloud light dissolving all structure and identity below — only velocity and scale are given — identity is withheld. Object motion registers below. Golden dust motes floating in angled afternoon light. Crowd looking skyward in a collective upward reaction.
[1.5–3.5s] Fast aggressive push — hard 60-degree banking turn through warming golden light through clearing atmosphere, cloud wisps clearing at frame edge. Vessel silhouette emerging through atmosphere — vessel silhouette emerging in mid-depth. Exhaust or rotor wash flattens vegetation below — loose particles stream in the slipstream.
[3.5–5.5s] Near-static hold — camera pierces cloud base in bright warm golden afternoon light — slow close orbit at surface height as the hull waterline dominates — nothing else competes. Rocket surface texture, defining lines, and wake trail fully visible at eye-level — hull and deck at natural depth. Subject shadow moves across ground — terrain features pass in sharp detail.
[5.5–8s] Camera decelerates abruptly, holds — camera pulls back 300m in rich golden hour light — the horizon line draws attention to the full scale of things. Rocket becomes a point of beauty against infinite horizon — infinite ocean surface and sky filling frame. Landscape scale revealed — shadows of clouds move across terrain — distant motion adds depth.
[8–10s] Near-static hold — camera holds wide in softening warm light — the vanishing point holds until the scene closes. Rocket rests in natural environment — ocean fills entire frame. Environmental disturbance settles — natural ground motion resumes. A visible wave of reaction moving through the stands.

ENVIRONMENT
Golden dust motes floating in angled afternoon light. Long shadows extending from every figure. Hair and loose fabric lift slightly in the afternoon breeze. Crowd looking skyward in a collective upward reaction. A visible wave of reaction moving through the stands.

STYLE
Hyperrealistic, photographic quality, professional cinema camera. Minimal motion blur.

CONTINUITY
