CAMERA

News anchor at maximum expression — full subject at natural depth. Environmental depth — background motion provides spatial context for the subject.

News anchor completes the arc — complete environmental scale. Full spatial scale — secondary motion reveals depth and distance relationships. Crowd murmur building into a growing roar.
